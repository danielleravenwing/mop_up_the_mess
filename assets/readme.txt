Mop Up the Mess 
by R. Ironwing and JED
Mutant Python Games (TM)

Based on the 1980's C64/Qbasic games by J.D.

The goal of the game is to mop up the mess before it kills the princess.
There are 100 levels.
Click the sponge to pick it up and click again to drop it.
Click bombs to set them off.

Enjoy
